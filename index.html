BATH SPA UNIVERSITY ACADEMIC CENTRE RAK

BSU RAK offers high quality teaching across a range of disciplines following the UK campus as being a leading university for Creativity, Culture, and Enterprise. It offers an innovative and up-to-date curriculum that meets the needs of the industry and the students.

WHY CHOOSE US?

Bath Spa University is where creative minds meet. We teach and research across Arts, Sciences, Education, and Business. The University employs outstanding creative professionals who support its aim to be a leading educational institution in creativity, culture, and enterprise.

INTERNATIONALLY ACCREDITED UK DEGREE

Bath Spa University, UK is a member of the British Quality Assurance Agency for Higher Education and meets all the desired requirements for foreign degree attestations and WES equivalency. BSU-UK shall solely administer the issuance of the degree and mark sheets.

HIGH QUALITY BRITISH EDUCATION

In order to ensure world-class education, Bath Spa University's thrust is to focus on developing four major themes of work as articulated in its Strategy 2030: Creativity, Enterprise, Talent, and Enhancements.

HIGHLY AFFORDABLE FEE STRUCTURE

Bath Spa University is committed to providing affordable fees through various discounts and scholarships such as merit-based, need-based, among many others for diverse students from different nationalities.

OPTION TO TRANSFER AND GRADUATE IN UK

Bath Spa University Academic Centre RAK students have the option to transfer and graduate on the UK campus. The university will assist the students to ensure the smooth processing of requirements.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is Bath Spa University Academic Centre RAK licensed and recognised?

Bath Spa University Academic Centre RAK is an approved institution by Bath Spa University UK campus to operate in the United Arab Emirates. It is also licensed by the Ras Al Khaimah Economic Zone (RAKEZ) to operate as a university through the RAKEZ Academic Zone. Bath Spa University UK meets all the desired requirements for foreign degree attestations. Further, the degree meets the WES Canada and America's equivalency requirements.

Will my degree be awarded by the UAE or UK?

The degree will be issued by Bath Spa University United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland.

Does the University provide any scholarship?

Yes, the University provides scholarships like merit-based, need-based among many others.

Does the university provide student visa?

Yes, student visa is available for students.

Can I transfer to Bath Spa University UK and continue my studies there?

Yes, the students can transfer to UK and complete their studies there.

How do I apply for the scholarship programme?

The University encourages student applicants to explore the website where scholarship application is available. To know more about the scholarship program, please send an email to admissions@bathspa.ae or contact us at 07 236 9495. To register for the scholarship exam, please apply here.

Is there a study tour programme in the UK?

Yes, there is a study tour being organised every year. The University facilitates the visa requirements of the students.
